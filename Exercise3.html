How to Make a Cream Puff

Choux Pastry

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup Butter
  • 1 cup Water
  • 1 teaspoon Brown Sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 5 large Eggs
  • 1 cup All Purpose Flour
  • 1 teaspoon Vanilla

Procedure:

  1. In a saucepan over medium-high heat, combine butter, sugar, salt and water.
  2. Bring to boil then add the flour.
  3. Cool down for 3 minutes, then add egg one at a time.

Fillings

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups Milk
  • 1/2 cup White Sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon Salt
  • 1 teaspoon Vanilla
  • 1/4 cup Cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon All Purpose Flour
  • 4 large Egg Yolks
  • 1/4 cup Butter

Procedure:

  1. In a saucepan mix 2&1/2 cup milk, suagr, salt and vanilla.
  2. Meanwhile, whisk cornstarch, flur, egg yolks, and 1/2 cup milk.
  3. Mix the two mixtures together then bring to boil.
  4. Strain the mixture then add butter.

Filling up the Coux Pastry

  1. Get a piping bag then put a small round pastry tip.
  2. Fill the bag with the fillings. *Fill the bag properly that there should be no air inside
  3. Using the small round pastry tip poke the bottom of the choux pastry
  4. Pipe the bag to fill up the coux pastry. *Be carefull not to overflow the fillings
  5. Pu it on a plate and design if you want, then serve.
